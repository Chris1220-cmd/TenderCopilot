## 1. Competitor Landscape

### Tier 1 - Direct Greek Market Competitors (Highest Priority)

These competitors operate specifically in the Greek public procurement space. Pages targeting these will have the highest conversion potential.

#### 1.1 e-tenders.gr
- **URL:** https://e-tenders.gr
- **Type:** Service provider (tender monitoring + consulting)
- **Since:** 2007
- **Positioning:** "Public Procurement Services since 2007" - ESIDIS specialists
- **Services:** Tender monitoring (from EUR 22.50/month), ESIDIS registration support, bid writing services, legal support
- **Subscription Tiers:** MINI, START, PREMIUM
- **Strengths:** Established brand, deep ESIDIS expertise, low entry price
- **Weaknesses:** Service-based (not SaaS), no AI, no document analysis, manual processes
- **SEO Presence:** Ranks for Greek-language ESIDIS queries
- **TenderCopilot Advantage:** Full SaaS platform vs. manual service; AI document analysis; eligibility checking; 24/7 availability

#### 1.2 iSupplies.gr
- **URL:** https://isupplies.gr
- **Type:** e-Procurement platform (buyer-side focus)
- **Positioning:** "Platform for Electronic Tenders" for public/private sector
- **Used By:** Hospitals (PAGNI, Agios Nikolaos), municipalities (Arta), ferries (Minoan Lines), health regions (4th YPE)
- **Pricing:** Free for suppliers; paid for procuring entities
- **Certifications:** ISO 9001:2015, ISO 27001
- **Strengths:** Widely deployed in Greek public sector, free for bidders, ISO certified
- **Weaknesses:** Buyer-side tool (not bidder-assistance), no AI, no document analysis, no eligibility checking
- **SEO Presence:** Strong in Greek procurement queries
- **TenderCopilot Advantage:** Bidder-focused vs. buyer-focused; AI-powered analysis; proposal generation; discovery engine

#### 1.3 promitheies.gr
- **URL:** https://www.promitheies.gr
- **Type:** Tender aggregation / information service
- **Positioning:** "The comprehensive platform for public tenders in Greece" - market leader in tender info
- **Data:** 460,000+ tender notices from 1,000+ sources
- **Strengths:** Largest Greek tender database, comprehensive coverage, established brand
- **Weaknesses:** Information-only (no bid management), no AI analysis, no eligibility checking, no proposal tools
- **SEO Presence:** Very strong - ranks #1 for many Greek tender queries
- **TenderCopilot Advantage:** End-to-end platform vs. information service; AI analysis; bid preparation; eligibility scoring

#### 1.4 diagonismoi-dimosiou.gr / dda.com.gr
- **URL:** https://diagonismoi-dimosiou.gr / https://dda.com.gr
- **Type:** Tender notification/monitoring service
- **Positioning:** "Public Tenders Information Service" with nationwide coverage
- **Coverage:** 6,000+ Greek public agencies, ~2,500 announcements/day
- **Subscription Tiers:** dd MINI, dd START, dd PREMIUM (3-month, 6-month, annual)
- **Sources:** Diavgeia, ESIDIS, KIMDIS, TED + private sources
- **Strengths:** Comprehensive coverage, flexible subscriptions, EU-wide data via TED
- **Weaknesses:** Notification-only service, no AI, no bid management, no analysis tools
- **SEO Presence:** Moderate for Greek procurement queries
- **TenderCopilot Advantage:** Full workflow management vs. notifications; AI assistant; proposal generation; eligibility checking

#### 1.5 contracts.gr
- **URL:** https://www.contracts.gr
- **Type:** Tender listing/notification portal
- **Positioning:** "Public Sector Tenders - Continuous Updates"
- **Strengths:** Simple interface, categorized by sector (software, services, etc.)
- **Weaknesses:** Basic listing, no management tools, no AI features
- **SEO Presence:** Moderate for specific sector tender searches
- **TenderCopilot Advantage:** Complete platform vs. listing board; AI analysis; eligibility; proposal generation

### Tier 2 - European/International AI Tender Platforms (Medium Priority)

These are SaaS competitors with overlapping features. Comparison pages will attract users evaluating modern solutions.

#### 2.1 Tendium
- **URL:** https://tendium.ai
- **Type:** AI-powered tender management SaaS (Nordics-based)
- **Positioning:** "Grow your public sector sales. Skip the hassle."
- **Features:** AI monitoring, collaborative bid creation, automated response generation, analytics
- **Target:** Medium-to-large enterprises (IT, construction, healthcare)
- **Markets:** Northern Europe primarily (Sweden HQ)
- **Pricing:** Tiered plans, not publicly disclosed
- **Strengths:** AI-first approach, strong European presence, mature product
- **Weaknesses:** No Greek language support, not ESIDIS-integrated, Nordic-centric, premium pricing
- **SEO Presence:** Strong for "tender management software" in English
- **TenderCopilot Advantage:** Greek language native; ESIDIS integration; Greek legal framework knowledge; local pricing

#### 2.2 Altura.io
- **URL:** https://altura.io
- **Type:** AI bid management platform
- **Positioning:** "Bid management platform with AI-driven features"
- **Features:** Opportunity qualification, bid planning, AI proposal writing, performance reports
- **Markets:** Netherlands, Belgium, UK
- **Pricing:** Yearly subscription, custom pricing, ISO 27001:2022, GDPR compliant
- **Strengths:** Deep AI capabilities, requirement mapping, EEA-hosted
- **Weaknesses:** No Greek market presence, no ESIDIS integration, limited to NL/BE/UK tender sources
- **SEO Presence:** Strong for "bid management software" in English
- **TenderCopilot Advantage:** Greek market specialization; ESIDIS native; Greek language; local compliance knowledge

#### 2.3 Tenderbolt.ai
- **URL:** https://www.tenderbolt.ai
- **Type:** AI tender response automation
- **Positioning:** "Respond to tenders with AI"
- **Features:** Document analysis, automated response generation, Excel questionnaire handling
- **Claims:** 70% time savings
- **Markets:** French-origin, European focus
- **Strengths:** Strong AI response generation, multi-format support
- **Weaknesses:** No Greek language, no ESIDIS integration, response-only (not full lifecycle)
- **SEO Presence:** Growing for AI tender queries
- **TenderCopilot Advantage:** Full lifecycle vs. response-only; Greek native; ESIDIS integration; discovery engine

#### 2.4 Brainial
- **URL:** https://brainial.com
- **Type:** AI tender management tool
- **Positioning:** "AI tool for tender management"
- **Features:** Auto requirement/risk identification, explainable AI, tone-matched writing, source references
- **Funding:** Backed by Pieter Schoen (Utrecht-based)
- **Strengths:** Explainable AI (shows sources), risk/red flag detection, style matching
- **Weaknesses:** No Greek market support, no ESIDIS integration, Dutch market focus
- **SEO Presence:** Moderate for AI tender queries
- **TenderCopilot Advantage:** Greek specialization; ESIDIS integration; discovery engine; eligibility scoring

#### 2.5 AITenders
- **URL:** https://aitenders.com
- **Type:** AI tender & contract management
- **Positioning:** "Tenders & Contract Management"
- **Features:** AI criteria extraction, MS Word add-in (Smart Draft), cross-team review alignment
- **Strengths:** Word integration, team collaboration, past-proposal learning
- **Weaknesses:** No Greek language, no ESIDIS integration, no discovery engine
- **SEO Presence:** Moderate
- **TenderCopilot Advantage:** Greek native; ESIDIS; discovery engine; web-based (no Word dependency)

#### 2.6 TenderFacts.ai
- **URL:** https://tenderfacts.ai
- **Type:** AI tender management (startup)
- **Positioning:** "The Future of AI-Assisted Tender Management"
- **Features:** Multi-model AI (GPT-4, Gemini, Claude, Llama, Mistral), rapid RFP insight extraction, proposal crafting
- **Founded:** 2023, Paris, unfunded
- **Strengths:** Multi-model AI approach, rapid document analysis
- **Weaknesses:** Early-stage startup, unfunded, no Greek support, limited track record
- **SEO Presence:** Low
- **TenderCopilot Advantage:** Greek specialization; established feature set; ESIDIS; discovery engine; eligibility scoring

#### 2.7 Stotles
- **URL:** https://www.stotles.com
- **Type:** Public procurement intelligence platform
- **Positioning:** "Win public sector contracts & simplify government tendering"
- **Features:** Tender discovery, AI qualification, buyer intelligence, CRM integrations (Salesforce, HubSpot)
- **Markets:** UK, Ireland primarily
- **Pricing:** Free core tier + paid tiers (3 levels)
- **Strengths:** Strong buyer intelligence, CRM integrations, free tier, large dataset
